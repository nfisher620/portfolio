AboutMe

Developer

As a teaching assistant at UC Davis for a course in "Technology in Computers", I was first introduced to coding by teaching students how to create personal websites for themselves. After graduating college, coding came back into my life after my sister went to Dev Bootcamp in San Francisco. She has inspired me to become a developer and I'm so excited to be following in her footsteps. With the support of my husband and family I went through a 13 week bootcamp at LearningFuze. I am beyond excited to start working as a front-end developer!.

California Dreamer

My name is Nicole and I was born and raised in Orange County, California. I graduated from the University of California at Davis in 2011 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Managerial Economics. In June 2015, I received a Business Analyst certificate from the University of California at Irvine. I am the youngest daughter of a family of four. My older sister and my parents mean the world to me and have made me the person I am today. I recently married the love of my life, Ben, this past summer in San Clemente. My favorite things to do are spend time with my husband and family, go to Disneyland, and bake desserts.

Disney Lover

To say I love Disneyland and anything Disney would be an understatement. Since I was 3 years old, there has been a tradition for my family and I to go to Disneyland every New Year's day. I've been a passholder for 8 years and have been to the parks over 200 times.. you definitely don't need a park map when you're with me! Churros and Dole Whip are my favorite treats and my favorite ride is Indiana Jones. My passion for Disney shows through some of my projects so don't be surprised if you find yourself looking at a Disney/Pixar movie themed project!
